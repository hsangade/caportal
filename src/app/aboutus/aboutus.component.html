Our Story

In this digital era, businesses need integrated tools that help them grow successfully. Corefactors was found in 2013 to create engineered marketing and communication solutions. Looking at the customer shift and how businesses struggle to keep up their customer base, Teleduce was born. Today teleduce is helping 4000+ users across India to sell faster and scale faster.
